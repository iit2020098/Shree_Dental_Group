Our Services

Teeth Cleaning

A professional cleaning of the teeth to remove plaque, tartar, and stains.

Tooth Whitening

A cosmetic dental procedure that involves the use of a bleaching agent to make teeth whiter and brighter.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a popular and effective way to restore missing teeth and improve your smile.

TJM- Joint point to Head

TMJ surgery corrects jaw joint issues to relieve pain and improve function, performed by an oral and maxillofacial surgeon.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry can enhance the appearance of your teeth and improve your smile through various procedures such as teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding.

Gum Disease Treatment

Gum disease treatment aims to prevent, diagnose, and treat infections and inflammation of the gums and supporting structures of the teeth, which can cause tooth loss if not managed.

Dental Surgery

Dental surgery refers to a range of dental procedures that involve surgical techniques, such as tooth extraction, dental implant placement, and corrective jaw surgery.

Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontic treatment involves correcting misaligned teeth and jaws using braces, clear aligners, or other dental appliances to improve dental function and aesthetics.

Child Dentistry

Child dentistry or pediatric dentistry aims to promote dental health for infants, children, and adolescents through preventative care and treatment.

Laser Dentistry

Laser dentistry is a safe and precise dental treatment option that uses a laser to perform a variety of procedures, including gum reshaping, cavity treatment, and teeth whitening.

Dental Filling

Dental filling is a dental procedure where a decayed or damaged tooth is restored by removing the affected area and filling it with a dental material, such as composite resin or amalgam.

Wisdom Tooth removal

Wisdom tooth removal is the extraction of the third molars located at the back of the mouth, often performed due to overcrowding or impaction issues.

Root Canal

Root canal treatment removes infected tooth pulp, cleans and disinfects the area, and fills it to prevent further infection.

Full Mouth Rehabitation

Full mouth rehabilitation involves restoring or replacing several damaged or missing teeth to improve the overall function and appearance of the mouth.

Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction is a dental procedure where a tooth is removed from its socket in the bone, typically due to damage, decay, or overcrowding.
